Conflict Management Consultancy & Training by

Helping government and non-government institutions with conflict management strategy, in both aspects of planing and management.

Building skills through our conflict management simulation training programs and promote human rights in conflict situation.

Education Development:

Assisting in the development of Education Services by enhancing knowledge transfer methods and building capacity, and conducting surveys and assessment for educational institution and raising different critical issues to the government.

Providing help and support with supplying necessary educational aids.

Respond to Crises with Relief and Humanitarian Aids

Our NGO is ready to respond to crises and provide Humanitarian aids. This could be in the form of food and life essential items.

Conducting humanitarian aid assessments before supplying aids and also provide reports and share it with other NGOs.

We have experience in supplying relief and humanitarian aid. We have severed more than 3500 displaced families as response to emergency of war crises started in Libya on August 2014.

Health Development, by

Providing training to medical and management staff.

Carrying out assessment for medical services and its infrastructure.

Providing advice and reports to improve health services.

Bringing in doctors for short/long term health service.

Working close with special needs to improve their life style.

Migration & Refugee managements, by

Providing assistance to migrants who illegally enter the country as well as refugees, by offering food, shelter and helping migration control officials with advise, plan and management of migration situation.

providing training for people to improve migrants and refugees situation by carrying assessments and research and produce reports to governmental and non-governmental institutions.

Prompting rights of migrants, refugees and internally displaced persons.

NGO & Youth Development

Conducting campaigns and workshops to raise environment and sustainable development awareness specially after war.

Supporting municipalities to gain skill of development planning and strategy for spatial development.
