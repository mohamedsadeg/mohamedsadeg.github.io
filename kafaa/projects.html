Distribution of Relief

In response to crises started in July 2014, Kafaa Development Foundation started distributions of humanitarian aids in form of food and life essentials to more than 3000 families located in different cities. The project was funded by Al Madar Al Jadeed and LITC and other donators.

Distribution of EID Cloths

This project was initiated to distribute new clothes to kids of displaced families to celebrate EID FITER 2014. The project was funded by Libya International Telecom Company. We have distributed thousands of new clothes to children located in some cities such Tripoli, Yefren, Derj, Ghadams, Ubary, Haraba, Gaser Ben Gasheer.

Assessment of Educational Institution in Rural Cities

Kafaa Development Foundation has conducted school assessment in some rural areas. The assessment has shown that those schools require some urgent needs such as the maintenance of school facilities and teaching facilities. Also installing heaters and computer and scientific labs and providing healthy water

Supply and Installation of Playgrounds

KDF has supplied and installed playgrounds for children after war. The aim of this project is to help children psychologically overcome the war trauma.

Distribution of EID Cloths

Kafaa Development Foundation has distributed more than 300 wheelchairs which were donated by Wheelchairs for Kids Charity. The project has reached disabilities children located in more than 20 cities.
