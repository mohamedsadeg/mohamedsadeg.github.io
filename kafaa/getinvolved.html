Kafaa Development Foundation welcome persons who wish to add values to its development strategy.
We are seeking volunteers to help with deploying our projects. We also seek professional people to contribute to our community and development projects.

As we are working in different disciplines, the following list of qualification is always required

  • Humanitarian Workers
  • NGO Capacity Building Trainers
  • English Teachers
  • Environment Specialist
  • Educations Professionals
  • Health
    professionals
  • Program Managers
  • Accountants
  • Migration Management Consultancy Specialties

We are also seeking volunteering trainers to conduct training in the following topics.

  • • NGO Management & leadership
  • • NGO Financing Management
  • • Proposal and Report Writing
  • • Project Management
  • • Monitoring and evaluation

To join us and become involved, please contact us at info@kafaa.ly
